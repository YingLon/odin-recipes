Feijoada

Brazillian feijoada, a sunday classic!

Brazil's national dish, loved by everyone. It is a stew loaded with black beans and meats of every description: smoked pork loin, bacon and sausage such as chorizo.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 3 garlic cloves, thinly sliced
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 1/2 pounds dried black beans, soaked overnight and drained

  • 1 dried red chile
